Consulting

I’m brought in when the problem isn’t clean, the constraints are real, and decisions need to hold up under scrutiny.

Why I’m brought in

  • Fundraising decisions that must align with reality
  • Operational drag slowing execution
  • Unclear priorities across teams or systems
  • AI or digital initiatives that need real leverage
  • Executives needing an external thinking partner

How I work

  • Break complex problems into solvable segments
  • Identify the true constraint, not the loudest symptom
  • Quantify trade-offs and decision impact
  • Stay close to execution, not just strategy

Engagement models

Advisory / Retainer

Ongoing judgment and prioritisation.

Fixed Project

Short, focused problem resolution.

Fractional / Interim

Leadership capacity during transition.

Targeted Sessions

Fast clarity on a defined issue.

Exploratory session

Exploratory sessions are reserved for people actively working through a real problem. Instead of a fee, I ask for a $50–$100 donation to a selected charity.
