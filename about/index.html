Career arc

I started working early personal training straight out of High School, then worked in the Security Division of a NSW Government Organistion. I studied while working, and acquired a range of transferrable skills across startups and complex environments.

I was headhunted by Apple on the Special Projects team to manage APAC R&D for the Apple Watch; I later spent time on the Investment team at Venture Fund, completed an MBA, and worked at BCG Digital Ventures.

With nearly 20 years in the tech industry, I’ve worked with folks at all levels, and operated across individual contributor, management, advisory, project work, and fractional roles.
